> Will he become a legend or just another cautionary tale?

MEET THE EDGERUNNERS

DAVID MARTINEZ

Protagonist

A street kid who becomes an Edgerunner after installing military-grade cyberware. His journey from nobody to legend defines the series.

Sandevistan Determined Ambitious

LUCY

Netrunner

A skilled netrunner with a mysterious past. She forms a close bond with David and has her own dreams of escaping Night City.

Hacking Independent Mysterious

MAINE

Leader

The leader of the Edgerunners crew that David joins. A veteran mercenary with a strong sense of loyalty to his team.

Strength Loyal Protective

REBECCA

Gun Nut

A loud, violent, and energetic member of the crew who specializes in heavy weapons. Despite her rough exterior, she cares deeply for her friends.

NIGHT CITY

THE CITY OF DREAMS

Night City is a sprawling metropolis where megacorporations rule and street gangs fight for territory. It's a place where dreams are made and broken every day.

CYBERWARE

In this future, cybernetic enhancements are commonplace. From basic implants to full-body conversions, cyberware defines what it means to be human.

EDGERUNNERS

Mercenaries living on the edge of society. They take the jobs no one else will, risking cyberpsychosis and death for money and glory.

LIVE FAST, DIE YOUNG

In Night City, the only way to survive is to become someone else. The Edgerunners live by their own code, taking on dangerous missions for the right price.

But every enhancement comes with a risk. Cyberpsychosis lurks around every corner, and the line between man and machine is thinner than ever.
